QUESTIONS WE GET

Refrigerated trucking,
answered.

Quick answers to what shippers and drivers most often ask. Need something specific — call 801-260-7010 and a real person picks up.

What does Simon Express haul?

+

Simon Express hauls only food and food-grade freight in temperature-controlled 53-foot reefer trailers — produce, dairy, protein and meat, frozen goods, beverages, and refrigerated CPG. Set-point range is −20°F to 70°F with continuous temperature monitoring. We do not haul household goods, chemicals, or building materials.

What states do you serve?

+

All 48 states, with daily lanes coast to coast. Common lane corridors include the Pacific Northwest to Florida, California to the Carolinas, the Upper Midwest to the East Coast, and the Texas triangle. Operations are headquartered in Salt Lake City, Utah.

Is Simon Express hiring Class A CDL drivers?

+

Yes — actively hiring Class A CDL drivers nationwide, and driver-nominated for Best Fleets to Drive For five years running (2022–2026). Top-tier pay, consistent reefer miles week after week, late-model Freightliner Cascadias, full benefits with 401(k) match after 90 days, real home time, paid orientation, referral bonuses, and pet- and rider-friendly. See careers or call 801-260-7010.

What temperature ranges can you maintain?

+

Set-point from −20°F frozen to 70°F ambient on every trailer, with continuous temperature monitoring. Reefer telemetry alerts dispatch the moment a unit drifts off set-point. Multi-temp dual-zone trailers are available for split-load freight.

Do you offer dedicated lanes?

+

Yes. Dedicated lanes mean running the same customer and the same route, week after week — predictable miles, predictable schedules, and home time you can plan around. Drivers who prefer a steady rhythm over rotating freight tend to gravitate toward dedicated.

What equipment do you run?

+

Late-model Freightliner Cascadia tractors with Detroit DD15 engines and automatic transmissions, paired with 53-foot Utility trailers and Carrier reefers featuring iBright and Lynx Fleet telematics.

Are you FSMA and FMCSA compliant?

+

Yes. Simon Express meets or exceeds FMCSA, DOT, and FDA Food Safety Modernization Act (FSMA) standards for refrigerated food transportation. ELD-compliant, sanitary trailer protocols, SmartWay carrier, fully insured.

How do I get a freight quote?

+

Request a quote at simonexpress.com/quote, email info@simonexpress.com, or call dispatch at 801-260-7010. Dispatch is available 24/7 — fully staffed seven days a week during the day, on call after hours, and a person always picks up. Quote responses come back within one business day; faster by phone.
