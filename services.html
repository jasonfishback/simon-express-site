Cold chain.
Coast to coast.

Simon Express provides national refrigerated trucking services across 48 states. We operate 53-foot reefer trailers hauling temperature-controlled food freight — produce, dairy, protein, frozen, and beverage — with continuous monitoring from origin to dock. Full truckload, dedicated lanes, and team-driver options available. 24/7 dispatch.

01 — TEMPERATURE CONTROLLED

Set-point holds
from origin to dock.

Multi-temp 53-foot reefer trailers with continuous temperature monitoring and tamper-evident seals. We pull temp logs at every key — origin, fuel, rest, destination — and we hand them to you. If the load needs to be cold, it stays cold. If it needs to be frozen, it stays frozen.

  • −20°F to 70°F set-point range
  • Continuous temperature monitoring
  • Sealed at origin, scanned at destination
  • Pre-cooled before live load
  • Multi-temp capable on dual-zone units
  • Reefer download on every drop
02 — REFRIGERATED FOOD

Food is all
we haul.

We don't dabble in chemicals, we don't haul building materials, we don't do household goods. We move food — coast to coast, every day. Produce, dairy, protein, frozen, beverage, refrigerated CPG. Lanes from California to the Carolinas, the Pacific Northwest to South Florida. Drivers trained on cold-chain protocols, dispatchers who know what "wet ice" and "tail temp" actually mean.

  • Produce — leafy greens, berries, root
  • Dairy — fluid, cultured, cheese
  • Protein — boxed beef, pork, poultry
  • Frozen — finished goods and bulk
  • Beverage — DSD and full-pallet
  • Specialty CPG — refrigerated and shelf-stable food
03 — FULL TRUCKLOAD

One trailer.
One driver.
Door to door.

Dedicated 53-foot reefers — no transfers, no intermediate handling, no LTL surprises. Team-driver options for time-critical lanes and West Coast turnarounds. We accept tenders fast, we pick up on time, and our on-time delivery sits at 99.2%.

  • 53-foot reefer trailers
  • Solo or team driver
  • Live-load and drop-and-hook
  • Coast-to-coast lanes
  • Power-only available on request
  • EDI / API tendering

Same trucks.

  • Single point of contact in dispatch
