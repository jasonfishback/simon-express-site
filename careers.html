DRIVER FAQ

What drivers
ask first.

The straight answers to the questions that matter before you sign on. More questions? Recruiting picks up at 801-260-7010.

Is Simon Express a good company to drive for?

+

Family-run national refrigerated carrier, driver-nominated for Best Fleets to Drive For five years running (2022–2026). Late-model Freightliner Cascadia tractors, consistent reefer freight year-round, top-tier mileage pay, full benefits with 401(k) match, paid orientation, referral bonuses, and real home time. Dispatch is available 24/7 — you reach a person, not a phone tree.

How much do Simon Express drivers make?

+

Top-tier mileage rates, competitive with the best fleets in the country. Call us with the number you're being offered elsewhere and we'll work to beat it. Specifics depend on experience level, home base, and lane preference. Recruiting at 801-260-7010.

What kind of trucks will I drive?

+

Late-model Freightliner Cascadia tractors with Detroit DD15 engines and automatic transmissions, paired with 53-foot Utility trailers and Carrier reefers featuring iBright and Lynx Fleet telematics. Strict PM schedule.

Are you pet- and rider-friendly?

+

Yes to both. Pets in the truck and passenger riders are welcome. Specifics on weight limits, breeds, and rider sign-up are walked through in orientation.

What experience do you require?

+

We require Class A CDL with at least 2 years of recent over-the-road experience. Clean MVR is preferred — but we'll talk to candidates with anything in their record. Context matters more than a checkbox.

What benefits come with the job?

+

Full medical, dental, and vision; 401(k) with company match after 90 days; paid orientation; referral bonuses; consistent weekly miles; pet- and rider-friendly; late-model equipment; real home-time scheduling.

What kind of home time can I expect?

+

Real home time — not the "we'll find you something" rhetoric you hear elsewhere. Specifics depend on the lane, but recruiting walks through realistic schedules during the call rather than after you sign on.
